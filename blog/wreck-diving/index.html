Wreck Diving

  • Pioneer

    The Pioneer wreck is a Japanese Warship, sunk in 1944. Pioneer is not actually the name of the vessel, but in the absence of identification this is the name which has been adopted. At a depth of 54 metres she is one of the deeper wrecks around Malapascua Island, something which has protected her from the local Hookah divers who have stripped several of the shallower wrecks in the area.

  • The Irako

    The Irako wreck is probably one of the best dive site of Coron in Palawan and of the Philippines. This 147 long cargo, was sunk by the american during the world war II. The wreck today is still intact and a lot of fish are swimming around. Scuba divers be ready to dive with school of barracudas and trevallies. The wreck is covered by colorfull corals and can be easily penetrate. We recommand you to be advanced scuba divers to do it !
