  • Pioneer Wreck Diving

    Another World War II wreck just off Malapascua Island near the larger island of Cebu, the Pioneer is not for the faint of heart. Starting at a narc-inducing 42 metres and descending to 54m, this is strictly for technical divers and is only accessed using trimix (oxygen, helium & nitrogen).
